# AI Usage

This repository is part of a broader AI-assisted development workflow documented in my public playbook: [Android AI Workflow Playbook](https://github.com/JaviGarcia1995/android-ai-workflow-playbook).

That playbook explains the general methodology, workflow structure, and validation rules used when working with AI tools.

In this project, AI was used as a supporting engineering assistant for:
- documentation drafting
- structuring technical explanations
- refining wording for public-facing case study content
- helping organize architecture and decision summaries

AI was not used to replace engineering judgment, final architecture decisions, or manual validation.

All project-specific decisions and final content were reviewed and adapted manually to match the actual implementation.
